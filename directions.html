Directions

From North Campus

Bus: take the 30, 32, 72, or 70 from Risley Hall, get off at Ithaca Commons - Seneca St.

Photo from Wikipedia

From West Campus

Bus: take the 70 from Baker Flagpole, get off at Ithaca Commons - Seneca St.

Walk: head toward the direction of Carraige House, turn right on either Buffalot St. or Seneca St. After a short walk down the hills, you should see the Commons.

From Collegetown

Bus: take the 10, 30, 32, 70, or 72 at Schwartz Performing Arts Center, get off at Ithaca Commons - Seneca St.

Walk: there are many ways to get to the Commons on foot. Walk down either E. Buffalo St. or E. Seneca St. If you want to more scenic route, walk down the Cascadilla Gorge Trail behind Schwartz Performing Arts Center, then cross N. Aurora St. to get to the Commons.
