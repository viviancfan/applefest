What is it?

About

Ithaca's annual Apple Harvest Festival is a yearly celebration of everything apple! Enjoy delicious food and drinks and watch performances by local talent. Come welcome fall with the Ithaca community and thousands of visitors from out of town. Featuring apples, local produce, fresh baked goods, games, live performances, and even prizes! Free to attend for all.
